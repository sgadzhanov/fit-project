Advanced training

You are advanced when you have to get pretty much everything in line to guarantee gains.

For the best long term hypertrophy results, advanced lifters should:

  1. Begin to focus on mind-muscle connection.

    2. Mind-muscle connection is only something that you can use when training hard, because a high level of tension or burn doesn't occur otherwise.

  2. Begin to focus on exercises that yield the best SFRs(Stimulus to Fatigue Ratio).

    3. You can also explore different exercises and try to pick the ones that have the best SFRs for you.

  3. Train mostly(50%) in the 10-20 rep range, with the rest of training in the 5-10 and 20-30 rep ranges.

    4. You have to take advantage of all rep ranges for maximum growth.

  4. Perfect pushing to failure while keeping a mind-muscle connection.

    5. You have to go to failure while keeping perfect form and make sure that the trained muscle is the limiting factor.

  5. Train to MRV(Maximum recoverable volume) most mesocycles before deloading, potentially starting at 2 RIR instead of 3-4, and then ending the final week beyond failure.

    6. Forced sets, dropsets are your best friends as an advanced individual at the end of a mesocycle.

  6. Consider either lots of prioritization phases or doing a few double daily sessions(6-10 sessions per week).

    7. You can no longer be doing full body training and still benefiting. You might choose to prioritize chest and back for example, while putting everything else on the backburner for a while, or you can do double sessions if your lifestyle and recovery is right for it.
